The Pirate's Code (The Rules)

There be real money in real woods, so there be real rules. Break 'em and ye be disqualified — and worse, ye be rude.

⏰
Hunt hours: 9:00 AM – 8:00 PM, daily. Be off the playing field by 8. There be campers sleepin' out here — no 2 a.m. prowlin' about with headlamps, ye night owls. Anyone caught huntin' after dark be disqualified (and walkin' the plank).
🌲

No cutting down trees

The forest stays standin'. Ye cannot chainsaw yer way to riches. The cash is not inside a tree.

🔥

No fires

Not one. No burnin' the brush to "smoke it out." We will find you, and so will the fire marshal.

⛳

No digging in the driving range

Leave the range as ye found it. No craters between the golfers. The loot is not under the range.

🚁

No trucks, excavators, or helicopters

Aye, someone asked. This is a hunt, not a demolition derby. Use yer eyes, yer boots, and yer wits.

🅿️

Park in designated areas

Stick to the marked lots and the playing field. Respect the neighbours, the fences, and the folks who live here.

🤝

Leave it better than ye found it

Pack out yer trash, be kind to the crew, and share the fun. That's the pirate code. Arrr.
