The Great Seba Beach Treasure Hunt

Legend says Captain Tim lost his wallet in the woods of Seba Beach — and what be a lost wallet, if not buried treasure? Grab yer map, ye landlubber, and plunder four legendary spots on the shores of Wabamun Lake.

The Legend of Tim's Lost Wallet

Every good treasure hunt needs a scallywag who lost something. Ours lost his wallet. Classic Tim.

Gather 'round, ye barnacle-brained beach bums. 'Twas a sunny day in Seba — as they always be — when ol' Captain Tim went a-wanderin' through the pines with pockets full of doubloons, loyalty punch-cards, and one very expired Blockbuster membership.

Somewhere betwixt the lodge, the lake, and a suspicious-lookin' squirrel, his trusty wallet slipped clean into the woods. Gone. Vanished. Swallowed by the forest like a doughnut into the sea.

Now the wallet be buried treasure, and the only way to find it is to visit the four legendary spots Tim swears he passed that day. Solve the clues, follow the map, and ye may just strike gold. (Or at least a really nice weekend by the lake.)

They say the Ghost of Captain Tim haunts the pier to this very day — moanin' not for revenge, nor for gold, but for the loyalty punch-card that was one stamp shy of a free coffee. Help him, ye good-hearted scallywag.

— The Ghost of Captain Tim 👻

How to Play (Arrr-ficial Rules)

No treasure-huntin' license required. Just vibes and maybe bug spray.

1

Study the Map

Open the treasure map above and scope out the four spots along Wabamun Lake. Memorize 'em or screenshot 'em, ye clever dog.

2

Crack the Clues

Each spot has a riddle. Solve it to figure out where to point yer boots (or yer minivan).

3

Visit the Spots

Roll up to SebaHub, SebaStays, Village Vows, and Kokanee Springs RV. Snap a photo. Look heroic.

4

Claim the Loot

Finish all four and Captain Tim's treasure be yours. (Prize details comin' soon — we're still diggin'.)
