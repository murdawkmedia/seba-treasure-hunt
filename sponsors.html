Questions from the evidence board

Before you send the note

The useful answer is usually a clear conversation, not a pre-set package.

Can we contribute products or services instead of cash?

Yes. Useful prizes, printing, services, and practical campaign support can be proposed through the inquiry form.

How flexible are sponsorship options?

They are flexible. Tell us what you can contribute and what a useful outcome would look like for your organization.

When could recognition be published?

Recognition is published only after both sides approve the details and timing in writing.

What happens after I submit?

A campaign operator will follow up privately to understand fit, timing, and next steps.

Does submitting an inquiry create a sponsorship agreement?

No. The campaign team reviews each inquiry privately. Recognition, deliverables, and publication require a separate agreement.
