Plain-language data practices

Privacy

The public hunt works without an account. When you choose member tools or send a report, this page explains what is collected and what stays private.

The short answer

Are my reports and exact location public?

No. Find, tip, and safety reports are private review records. Contact details, raw location, original images, and internal review notes are not placed on the public clue board automatically.

What works without an account

You can read the campaign story, public route overview, rules, official updates, and virtual clue board without registering. You can also submit a private report without a hunter account.

Hunter account information

If you create an optional account, the service stores your identity-provider account ID, verified email, private profile, generated public hunter handle, progress, Field Notes, reports, preferences, and consent history. A profile may include your name, optional phone, town or general area, broad age band, interests, and how you heard about the hunt.

Your legal name, email, phone, and private profile do not become your public clue-board name.

Reports and optional location

Reports collect the type of report, your contact details, the nearest waypoint when known, a location description, details, and images when supplied. A find claim requires photographic evidence.

Browser location is optional. The site asks for it only after you press the location button. If you do not press it, latitude and longitude are not added to the report.

Community Field Notes and images

Field Notes and their images are private while awaiting moderation. Approved public images are re-encoded to remove embedded metadata such as GPS information. Private originals are never used as the public image.

Short replies may appear immediately after automated checks. They use your generated hunter handle, not your private contact details, and remain subject to reporting and removal.

Human checks, security, and logs

Public submissions use a human-verification check and privacy-conscious rate limits to reduce abuse. Security records may include shortened or transformed network indicators, request identifiers, and authentication events. The service does not intentionally place full IP addresses, identity tokens, passwords, reset codes, raw human-check tokens, or private coordinates in application logs.

Marketing choices

Hunt updates, broader SebaHub marketing, and text-message marketing are separate choices. They start unchecked and are not required for Hunter Dashboard access. Text-message consent appears only when a phone number is supplied. You can change these preferences from your account.

Retention

  • Abandoned uploads are scheduled for removal after 24 hours.
  • Rejected or withdrawn submissions and private originals are scheduled for removal after 30 days.
  • Find evidence may be retained until 90 days after the case is resolved.
  • Published community content may remain through the campaign and for 90 days afterward.
  • Inactive account and security/audit records may be retained for 12 months, unless a consent withdrawal, legal requirement, or documented hold changes that period.

Your choices

You can use the hunt without an account, decline optional location, leave marketing boxes unchecked, remove your own community reply, and change account preferences. For access, correction, deletion, or consent questions, use the campaign contact channel shown on the main website and identify the account or report involved.
