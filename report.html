What happens next

A human reviews it

  1. Received: the system stores your report privately.
  2. Reviewing: an operator checks the details and evidence.
  3. Contacted or escalated: the team follows up when needed.
  4. Verified, rejected, or resolved: the review outcome is recorded.

A sanitized official update can be drafted later, but only through a separate operator decision.
