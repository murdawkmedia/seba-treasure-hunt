      Field safety

      Before you search

      • Search only between 9:00 AM and 8:00 PM when the case is open.
      • Follow visible restricted, hazardous, and temporarily closed labels.
      • Use designated parking and respect homes, guests, wildlife, and private property.
      • Do not dig, cut vegetation, light fires, or bring vehicles into search areas.

      Found something?

      Pause and document it

      Photograph the item where it is, note the nearest waypoint, and send a private find report. Avoid posting an exact location publicly.
